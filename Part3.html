A LOVE LETTER

by ryan

Dear M,
I miss you.
I made a mistake.
I’m sorry.
I should have fought with you. I was weak and I’m sorry. I was immature and afraid of getting close to you. I was afraid of getting hurt again. I was stupid and selfish. I didn’t want to get hurt so I hurt you instead. I got drunk and said things I didn’t mean and for that I am truly sorry.
I left at a low point in your life and when you asked if we could try again, I said no and I will never forgive myself for this. I know it’s too late to say this and I don’t expect you to forgive me. I just want you to know that I’m sorry.
I do hope that you’re doing well in college. I hope you find new friends, experience new things, and lastly I hope you’re happy ~R
